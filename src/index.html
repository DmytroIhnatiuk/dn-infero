Microsoft Power BI is a leading business analytics tool that transforms raw data into meaningful insights through interactive visualisations and real-time reports. Widely adopted by organisations globally, Power BI enables users to make data-driven decisions with ease.

Our Power BI Courses

Our Microsoft Power BI training is designed to help beginners become advanced users, providing comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience with Power BI’s powerful features. Learn to create stunning dashboards, generate reports, and easily connect to various data sources, all while enhancing your analytical skills.

Power BI integrates seamlessly with other Microsoft services, including Excel and Azure, offering a robust platform for data analysis and business intelligence. Empower your team to use data effectively and drive your organisation forward with Microsoft Power BI.

Power BI Certification

On completion of any of our Power BI training: Introduction and Pro you will receive the Certificate of Completion.

You can also receive CPD Certificate and points on completion of the Power BI Pro course.

In addition, our Power BI training is designed to prepare you for the Microsoft Certification – Power BI Data Analyst, which requires a delegate passing the exam:

PL-300: Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Associate Please note that the exams must be scheduled separately at an authorised local Pearson VUE Test Centre or taken online on Microsoft Certified: Power BI Data Analyst Associate certification page, as we do not offer Microsoft certification directly. The cost of the exam is typically around £113.
